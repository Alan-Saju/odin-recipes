Mint Chicken

Description

Mint Chicken is a tasty variety of Curry Chicken a dish most popular in India.

This recipe is originated from Northern India.

Ingredients

  • 2skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cut into bite-size pieces
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • ½ cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup margarine
  • ¼ cup fresh lemon juice
  • ½ cup chopped fresh mint leaves/li>

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, mix together the chicken pieces and garlic. Toss with the flour just to coat, and shake off excess.

  2. Heat the margarine in a large skillet over medium-high heat.

    Add the chicken pieces; cook and stir until browned.

    Add the mint leaves and lemon juice, cover and steam for about 5 minutes, just until the chicken is cooked through. Do not over cook the chicken or it will be tough.
